# Product Context

Paragliding is an exhilarating sport that requires careful planning and awareness of environmental conditions. Pilots need to consider flight restrictions, weather patterns, thermal hotspots, and optimal routes to ensure safe and enjoyable flights.

Tern serves as the digital flight deck for paragliders, providing all essential tools in one integrated application. The app must be usable both on the ground for pre-flight planning and in-flight for real-time navigation and decision making.

## Why Tern Exists
- Bridge the gap between simple paper charts and modern aviation flight decks
- Provide paragliders with professional-grade planning tools
- Enhance safety through comprehensive airspace visualization and alerts
- Enable better flight experiences through weather integration and route optimization

## How It Should Work
- **Pre-flight Planning**: Pilots can view airspaces, hotspots, weather forecasts, and plan routes
- **In-flight Navigation**: Real-time map display with GPS tracking, airspace warnings, and terrain awareness
- **Flight Phase Adaptation**: Adapts interface based on flight phase (Launch, Thermal, Glide, Landing)
- **Data Integration**: Pulls from multiple sources (NWS, OpenMeteo) for comprehensive weather data
- **Offline Capability**: Caching system allows access to critical data without cellular connectivity
- **User Customization**: Adjust measurement units, display preferences, and notification settings

## User Experience Goals
- Clean, uncluttered interface focused on flight information
- Reliable performance even with limited connectivity
- Intuitive controls that don't distract during flight activities
- Cross-platform consistency for users switching between devices
- Aviation-grade safety standards for competition pilots (Red Bull X-Alps)
- Clear prioritization of safety-critical information

## Paraglider-Specific Priorities
**🚨 PRIORITY 1: Never Reduce (Safety Critical)**
- Danger Areas, Restricted Areas, Temporary Restrictions, Parachute Zones

**⚠️ PRIORITY 2: High Priority (Flight Critical)**
- Training Areas, Competition Areas, Weather Avoidance, Thermal Sources

**📍 PRIORITY 3: Moderate Priority (Situational Awareness)**
- Controlled Airspace, Glider Sites, Terrain Hazards, Landing Options

**ℹ️ PRIORITY 4: Low Priority (Reduce First)**
- Airways, Reporting Points, Navigation Aids, Civil Airports
